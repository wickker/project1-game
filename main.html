CONWAY'S GAME OF LIFE

Rules

  • Every cell interacts with its eight neighbors. Live cells are black and dead cells are white.
  • Live cells with fewer than two live neighbors or more than three live neighbors will die.
  • Live cells with two or three live neighbors will live on to the next generation.
  • Dead cells with exactly three live neighbors will be 'reborn' as a live cells.
